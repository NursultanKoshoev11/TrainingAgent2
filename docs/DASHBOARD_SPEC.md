# Dashboard Specification

The dashboard should show analysis, not trading controls.

## Pages

## Overview

- Watchlist symbols.
- Recommendation label.
- Final score.
- Market score.
- Sentiment score.
- Risk score.
- Confidence.
- Quality flags.

## Asset Detail

- Latest price.
- Timeframe.
- Trend notes.
- Liquidity score.
- Event risk score.
- Recent news with sentiment.
- Risk flags.
- Signal history.

## News Feed

- Title.
- Source.
- Published date.
- Related assets.
- Sentiment label.
- Sentiment confidence.

## Signal History

- Saved advisory signals.
- Final score changes.
- Recommendation changes.
- Risk changes.

## Research Reports

- Backtest summaries.
- False-positive analysis.
- Market regime comparison.

## Forbidden UI

- No real order button.
- No private exchange key form.
- No balance transfer form.
- No leverage controls.
